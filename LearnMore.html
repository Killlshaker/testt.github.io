    Recreational Ice Rink

    A portable ice skating rink from Egypt Ice Rink is up to as much as 40% more energy efficient than other ice skating rink systems. For example Agyad Mall Ice Rink , Sun city Mall Ice Rink. Thanks to our 10 years of experience, Egypt Ice Rink is your partner of choice for a successful ice event.
